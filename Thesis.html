Impact of Microfinance on GDP

Microfinance institutions have emerged as key players in the global economy, empowering small entrepreneurs and increasing financial inclusion. This section details empirical findings on the relationship between microfinance and GDP growth.

Key Findings

  • Microfinance has contributed to increased household consumption and investment.
  • Higher access to credit boosts aggregate demand, leading to GDP growth.
  • Challenges include over-indebtedness and sustainability of microfinance programs.
